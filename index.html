English breakfast

A full breakfast is a substantial cooked breakfast meal, often served in the United Kingdom and Ireland, that typically includes back bacon, sausages, eggs, black pudding, baked beans, some form of potato, tomatoes, mushrooms, toast, and a beverage such as coffee or tea.

Japanese breakfast

Although a Japanese breakfast includes many things, it does not have to be heavy or overly filling. Portion sizes for breakfast are often small, and the dishes themselves are quite light, for example, they are not greasy or deep-fried.

France breakfast

A French breakfast is sweet. It is composed of slices of buttered bread and jam spread on it, sometimes croissants or other pastries and cereals.
